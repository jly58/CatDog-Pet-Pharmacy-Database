The Overview

The CatDog Pet Pharmacy, working closely with a network of 10 local Veterinarians, provides services to an average of 500 pet clients annually. These services are exclusively for owned pets; the pharmacy and its associated veterinarians do not cater to stray animals. Additionally, it is assumed that each pet client is assigned to only one veterinarian to ensure consistency and personalized care.The pharmacy offers a range of Medications, dispensing approximately 1,000 Prescriptions per year, generating an average annual revenue of $50,000. CatDog Pet Pharmacy hopes to deploy a website that will record prescriptions dispensed per year, number of prescriptions that are picked up, annual total revenue, and most frequently prescribed medications. The website will also document the number of Pets each veterinary clinic has with the pharmacy. Furthermore, the system lists the contact information for Veterinarians and Owners, medication information, and pet demographics. Since all clients are based in the United States, there is no need to consider international dialing codes for contact numbers. If the veterinarians are located abroad, they can be reached via their provided email addresses. With this website, CatDog Pet Pharmacy will be able to better understand and meet the needs of their clients. Note there are some limitations to our website. In a real world scenario, our database would have additional transaction type tables but for simplicity, cost is given for each set quantity of medication and a total prescription_cost is calculated for the cost of all medications in a prescription. Furthermore, our practice strives to uphold privacy practices, such that medication and prescription records are deleted completely for clients that we no longer serve.

Table Name Description
Vets Records the details of Vets that do business with us
Owners Records the details of Owners of our pet clients
Pets Records the details of Pets that we serve
Medications Records the details of Medications that our pharmacy dispenses
Prescriptions Records the details of Prescriptions that we issue
PrescriptionMedications Records the details of Prescriptions and Medications in an intersection table
